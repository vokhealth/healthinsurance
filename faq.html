Insurance 101

Medi-Share is a healthcare sharing ministry that allows members to share medical expenses. It is not traditional insurance but can be an alternative for some.

HMO plans require you to use doctors within a network and often need referrals to see specialists. PPO plans offer more flexibility with providers and don’t usually require referrals.

Open enrollment is a specific time period each year when you can sign up for or change your health insurance plan.

Special enrollment periods allow you to sign up for health insurance outside the open enrollment window due to qualifying life events like marriage, having a baby, or losing other coverage.
