Insurance 101

Medi-Share is a faith-based healthcare sharing ministry that allows members to share medical expenses with others. It is not traditional insurance but can be an alternative for some.

HMO (Health Maintenance Organization) plans usually require you to choose a primary care doctor and get referrals to see specialists. PPO (Preferred Provider Organization) plans offer more flexibility to see specialists without referrals and typically have a larger network.

Open Enrollment is the yearly period when you can sign up for or change health insurance plans without needing a special reason. Outside this time, you generally need a qualifying event to enroll.

Special Enrollment Periods are times outside Open Enrollment when you can sign up for or change plans due to qualifying life events like marriage, having a baby, or losing other coverage.
