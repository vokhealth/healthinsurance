Insurance 101

Medi-Share is a healthcare sharing ministry where members share medical expenses. It’s not traditional insurance but can be a lower-cost option for some healthy individuals.

HMOs require you to use a network of doctors and get referrals for specialists, usually at lower cost. PPOs give you more freedom to see any doctor, even out-of-network, but typically cost more.

Open enrollment is the yearly period when anyone can sign up for or change their health insurance plan without needing a special reason.

Special enrollment lets you sign up or change plans outside open enrollment if you’ve had certain life events, like getting married, having a baby, or losing other coverage.
