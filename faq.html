Frequently Asked Questions

Medi-Share is a healthcare sharing ministry where members share medical expenses. It’s not insurance but offers a community-based way to handle medical costs.

HMO plans require you to choose a primary care doctor and get referrals to see specialists. PPO plans offer more flexibility, letting you see any provider without referrals but usually cost more.

Open Enrollment is the yearly period when you can sign up for or change your health insurance plans without needing a special reason.

Special Enrollment allows you to sign up for or change your insurance outside the Open Enrollment period if you have qualifying life events like marriage, birth of a child, or loss of coverage.
