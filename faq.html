FAQ’s

Medi-Share is a Christian health-sharing program where members contribute monthly and share each other's medical costs. It’s not traditional insurance.

HMOs require you to stay within a provider network and need referrals. PPOs offer more flexibility in choosing providers and usually don’t require referrals.

Open Enrollment is the annual period when you can enroll in a health insurance plan—typically from Nov 1 to Jan 15.

You may qualify if you’ve had a major life event like losing other coverage, getting married, having a baby, or moving to a new ZIP code.
