Frequently Asked Questions

PPOs offer more doctor flexibility and don’t require referrals. HMOs are cheaper but have a smaller network and require referrals.

Open Enrollment is the set window (usually in the fall) where you can enroll or change health insurance plans.

You may qualify for Special Enrollment due to life events like moving, losing coverage, or getting married.

Medi-Share is a Christian health-sharing program. It’s not insurance, but members share each other’s medical expenses.
