FAQ’s

Answers to the most common health insurance questions and enrollment basics.

💡 What is Medi-Share?

Medi-Share is a Christian-based health cost sharing ministry. Members contribute monthly to help pay each other's medical bills. It's not traditional insurance and may not cover all needs.

🔍 What's the difference between HMO and PPO?

HMO plans require in-network providers and referrals. PPO plans offer flexibility, allowing visits to out-of-network doctors and no referral requirement.

🗓️ What is Open Enrollment?

This is the window (typically Nov 1–Jan 15) where you can sign up or switch ACA health plans. Outside this time, you’ll need a qualifying life event.

✅ What qualifies for Special Enrollment?

Special Enrollment is triggered by major life events—like losing coverage, marriage, divorce, childbirth, or relocation. You typically have 60 days to enroll.
